pizza!


description

this pizza recipe is like no other, it will make your tastebuds explode with joy from the first bite. a simple but delicious recipe which can be made in the short time of only 12 minutes.
It is also a great base for other pizzas, add whatever toppings you like and make it your own!

ingredients

  • pizza sauce
  • pizza dough
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese

steps

  1. On a lightly floured surface, stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a non-stick baking sheet.
  2. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge.
  3. Sprinkle with mozzarella.
  4. Sprinkle with parmesan before baking for 12 to 14 minutes, or until the crust is browned and the cheese is bubbling.
