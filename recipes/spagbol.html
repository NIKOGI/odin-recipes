spagbol!


description

Spagbol can be described in many different ways, but one way that it cant be described is bad. This spagbol recipe is super simple to make and will have you in tears of joy from just how good it is, A simple meal can truly go a long way.

ingredients

  • beef
  • thyme
  • bay leaves
  • sugar
  • beef cube
  • worcestershire sauce
  • onion and garlic
  • tomato sauce
  • red wine
  • canned tomatoes

steps

  1. Saute garlic and onion for about 3 minutes
  2. brown the beef for about 2 mins
  3. Add everything else, give it a good stir then simmer for 20 minutes minimum (midweek), up to 3 hours (weekend indulgence!). Slow cooking makes the beef incredibly tender and the sauce develops extra flavour.
